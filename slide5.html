Modern Architecture

Behavior Trees

Born in Halo 2 (2004)

Designed by Damian Isla to handle the chaotic combat of Elites and Grunts.

Hierarchical & Modular

Build complex behaviors by combining simple, reusable sub-trees.

Separation of Concerns

Decouples "what decision to make" (logic) from "how to execute it" (actions).

Tick-Based Execution

Traversed frame-by-frame, allowing graceful interruption and state handling.
